Roblox

Roblox is an online platform developed by Roblox Corporation that enables users to create and play games, referred to as "experiences." Launched in 2006, it has grown significantly, boasting an average of 85.3 million daily active users as of February 2025. The platform is particularly popular among children and teenagers, with half of all American children under 16 reportedly engaging with it
